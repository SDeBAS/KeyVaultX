About

KeyVaultx

KeyVaultX are used to securely store and manage keys for various applications, such as for buildings, vehicles, or storage units. They are designed to provide quick and convenient access to keys and track them while maintaining security. KeyVaultX allows for secure key management by only allowing authorized personnel to access the keys.


By KeyVaultX, keys can be quickly accessed and tracked without having to go through the hassle of searching for them or waiting for someone to unlock them.
